Some common questions
were often asked

What is the average time it takes to develop a software application or project with us?

The time required varies depending on the size and complexity of the project. We provide specific estimates based on project requirements after a thorough assessment of goals and expected duration.

How do we handle security and data protection issues in development processes?

We adhere to strict security practices including data encryption, risk assessment, and using best software development practices to ensure the protection of data and customer information.

Do we offer post-development support services?

Yes, we provide post-development support services including maintenance, software updates, and emergency issue resolution to ensure reliable application operation. These services are provided free of charge during the agreed trial period but become chargeable after the trial period ends.

Do we provide consultancy or assessment services for current software projects?

Yes, we offer consultancy services to evaluate current projects, identify strengths and weaknesses, and provide recommendations for improving performance and efficiency.

How do we determine the cost and timeline for a software development project?

We determine the cost and schedule based on a detailed analysis of your project requirements, quality factors, and the expected delivery timeline.

Do we cater to small and medium-sized companies or focus only on large corporations?

We work with companies of all sizes, including small, startup, medium-sized, and large enterprises, as well as individuals. Our goal is to effectively and appropriately meet the needs of all clients.
